## Risk Guidance
- **High-risk names (PLTR, CLNE, BB, SPCE, AMC):** Estimated profit/loss ranges are wide. Elevated volatility means realized outcomes may diverge substantially from estimates. Treat as small, speculative allocations only.
- **Low-risk names (BTC, ETH, SOFI, LINK, DOT, GME, SOL):** More stable but modest upside; still subject to broad market swings.
- **Cost drag:** Equities carry 0.25% vs. crypto 0.18%. This meaningfully erodes low-margin trades (e.g., SOL, GME net negative after cost).
- **Diversification:** Note concentration — several high-risk equities move on sentiment/meme dynamics, not fundamentals.

## Bottom-Line Summary
The most attractive *momentum* research cases are **PLTR** and **CLNE**, but only for high-risk-tolerant profiles. The best *risk-adjusted* candidates are **BTC, ETH, and SOFI**. **SPCE and BB** show the clearest downside bias and warrant avoidance. Because **all assets lack long-term trend confirmation**, this data supports short-term tactical views far more than long-term positioning.
